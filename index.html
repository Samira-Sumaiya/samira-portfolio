I'm Samira Sumaiya, a Frontend Designer who believes that beautiful code and thoughtful design aren't opposites — they're the same conversation. My journey started with a blank HTML file and a spark of curiosity that never went out.

I blend semantic HTML, expressive CSS, and dynamic JavaScript to build interfaces that feel as natural as breathing. With Node.js and MongoDB in my toolkit, I speak the full stack's language — making me a designer who can also ship the backend.

I live for the micro-details: the animation that makes a button feel alive, the gradient that gives a page its mood, the spacing that lets content breathe.
